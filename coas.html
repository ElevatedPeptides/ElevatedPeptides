ABOUT OUR TESTING

Our Testing Standards

  • HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) purity analysis on every batch
  • Mass spectrometry identity confirmation
  • Endotoxin / sterility testing where applicable
  • Results from independent, accredited third-party labs

Research Use Only

All Elevated Peptides compounds are sold strictly as research chemicals. COAs confirm compound identity and purity for laboratory research purposes. Not for human consumption. Not evaluated by the FDA.

Missing a COA?

If you don't see a COA for a compound you ordered, contact us — we'll get it to you directly. COAs are also included in packaging for select compounds.

📎 How COA Links Work

Each COA is a PDF stored in Google Drive. To share one, follow these steps:

1

Upload the PDF to Google Drive

2

Right-click the file → Share → under "General access" select "Anyone with the link"

3

Click Copy link — it will look like:
https://drive.google.com/file/d/XXXXX/view?usp=sharing

4

Send the link to whoever maintains the site so they can add it to the product data
